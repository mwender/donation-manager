How to Import Pick Up Providers

Step 1: Setup your CSV with the following columns (store_name, zipcode, email, receive_emails, and priority):

store_name zipcode email receive_emails priority
Non-Profit Store 12345 nonprofit@example.org 1 0
PRIORITY Pick Up Provider 98765 priority@example.com 1 1

Please note that the above table has one header row followed by two example rows. Don't include the examples in your CSV.

Notes
  • receive_emails (0 or 1) - 0 = false (i.e. don't receive emails), 1 = true (i.e. receive emails). If this is your first time uploading all the contacts in your CSV, then receive_emails should be set to 1 for all rows in your CSV.
  • priority (0 or 1) - 0 for non-profits, 1 for priority pick up providers.

Step 2: Upload your CSV via the Upload a CSV dialog below. After your CSV has uploaded, click on the button labeled Insert Into Post. Your new CSV will appear in the list below.

Step 3: Click on the title of your uploaded CSV to preview the first five rows. If everything looks "ok", click on the button to import the file. IMPORANT: Do not close your browser while the upload is working. Once your import has finished, you may delete your CSV.
